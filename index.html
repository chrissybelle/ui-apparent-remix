About Us

We at APParent believe that parents should spend more time with their children and less time looking for the goods and services needed to care for them. That is why we created APParent - a dynamic responsive platform bringing all your day-to-day needs in one place, at the click of a button. Need to find a nearby doctor? Need to find that special toy you little one has been asking for? Need to make family plans at the drop of a dime? We can help.

Let us do the searching; you have better things to do.
